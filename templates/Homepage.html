How CredibilityScan Assesses News Credibility

“From classification to verified evidence — CredibilityScan helps you identify trustworthy information in seconds.”

Credibility Scoring
Using the Random Forest classification algorithm, the system computes a credibility score ranging from 0% to 100%, reflecting the reliability level of the article.
Machine Learning Classification
The Random Forest model classifies online news as Real or Fake. This automated classification allows for consistent and scalable credibility assessment.
Cross-Referencing
The system conducts automated cross-referencing by comparing extracted claims and keywords from the submitted article against Reputable Philippine news online sources.
